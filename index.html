How to Use

📏 Una, ready your measuring tape!

BTU = tamang lakas ng aircon para sa size ng kwarto mo. Kailangan natin ng tamang sukat para maayos ang computation.

1. Client Information

Fill in the client's name, contact number, address, and survey date. Select the property type and area where the aircon will be installed.

2. Paano sukatin ang kwarto?

📐 Top-down view. Measure the two sides of the room — length (habang) and width (lapad).
  1. Length — Sukatin mula isang dingding hanggang sa kabilang dingding (habang ng kwarto).
  2. Width — Sukatin mula sa isang dulo hanggang sa kabila (lapad ng kwarto).
  3. Enter both in meters. Floor area computes automatically. (1 meter ≈ 3.28 ft)
💡 Tip: If you only have a tape measure in feet, divide by 3.28 to convert to meters. Example: 13 ft ÷ 3.28 ≈ 4m.

3. Ceiling Height

Standard ceiling height is 2.9m. If your ceiling is taller, enter only the excess.

Example: if your ceiling is 4 meters, enter 1 meter (4 − 2.9 ≈ 1.1). Adds 7% per meter.

4. Internal Heat Load (Optional)

For more accurate results, tap the Internal Heat Load card to expand it, then enter the quantity of heat-generating items (people, appliances, etc.). Skip this if you're unsure.

5. Environmental Factors

  1. Low Clearance — Toggle "Yes" if the gap between ceiling and roof is less than 1 meter (adds 15%).
  2. No Roof Insulation — Toggle "Yes" if the roof has no insulation (adds 20%).

6. Result & Price Options

The recommended HP is calculated as: Total BTU ÷ 9,000.

Tap any HP option card (Downsize / Recommended / Upsize) to see the price range and estimated monthly electricity cost for that capacity.

Tip: Use "Copy Details" to send a complete summary to AirconKing via Messenger.
