How to Use

1. Client Information

Fill in the client's name, contact number, address, and survey date. Select the property type and area where the aircon will be installed.

2. Room Measurement

  1. Enter the room's length and width in meters. The floor area calculates automatically.
  2. Toggle Direct Sunlight to "Yes" if the room gets direct sun exposure (adds 15%).
  3. If the ceiling is higher than the standard 2.9m, enter the excess meters (adds 7% per meter).

3. Internal Heat Load

Enter the quantity of each heat-producing item in the room (people, appliances, lights, etc.). Each item has a fixed BTU value that gets added to the total.

4. Environmental Factors

  1. Low Clearance — Toggle "Yes" if the gap between ceiling and roof is less than 1 meter (adds 15%).
  2. No Roof Insulation — Toggle "Yes" if the roof has no insulation (adds 20%).

5. Result

The recommended HP is calculated as: Total BTU ÷ 9,000. Use this as a guide to recommend the right aircon unit to the client.

Tip: Use the "Print / Save PDF" button to generate a professional summary you can share with the client.
